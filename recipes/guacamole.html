How to do Guacamole

Description

Just ripe avocados and a handful of flavorful mix-ins. Serve it as a dip at your next party or spoon it on top of tacos for an easy dinner upgrade.

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • ¼ cup chopped onion and ¼ cup chopped cilantro
  • Lime juice

Steps

  1. Mash avocados in a bowl until creamy.
  2. Mix tomatoes, onion, cilantro, lemon juice, add jalapeno pepper for better taste into mashed avocado until well combined; season with salt and black pepper.
